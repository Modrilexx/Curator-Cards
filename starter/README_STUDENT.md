Student Reflection


This project introduced the workflow of research, constraining AI output, drafting content, and verifying claims. Instead of using AI to generate final answers directly, I used a structured process to guide the results and improve accuracy.

One example where I challenged the AI was the founding date of the Bauhaus school. The AI identified 1919 as the founding year, but I verified this information using the Museum of Modern Art website before including it in the final curator card.

This process showed how AI can be useful for exploration and drafting, but verification is necessary before publishing information. The structured workflow helped keep the project organized and made the results more reliable.
